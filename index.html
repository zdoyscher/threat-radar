A quiet OpenAI news day changes none of it; the cheaper-tokens trend is doing the damage right now.

Named, live products undercut the model directly: Rocky.ai runs daily accountability, goal-setting, and executive role-play with free and low-cost tiers; Wysa offers an AI life-coach chat from $9.99/month with 150+ exercises; AI fitness coaches charge ~$20/month for unlimited access against $40–100 per session for a human trainer. IMPACT — WHAT IT MEANS FOR THE MONEY

When the marginal cost of the information you sell drops to zero, you can't price the information. Durable value shifts to what these apps can't replicate — accountability you're held to by a real person, personalized judgment, lived credibility, community, and presence — and away from "I'll teach you the framework." Coaches and educators who sell information rather than transformation are the most exposed of all.

Sell transformation and accountability — not information.

  3. Package a repeatable transformation system with measured outcomes. Build it like a product, deliver it like a high-touch relationship, measure it like a performance program. Short paid challenges (5–21 days) and paid communities convert and retain especially well.
REALITY CHECKThe pure info-product / course model is structurally challenged. If you sell knowledge, you have to move to selling change — a genuine reinvention of the offer, not a price change.
